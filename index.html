About The Server

THE BUNKER is a dark and immersive DayZ experience built for players who want true survival. Fight through terrifying infected creatures, raid underground bunkers, survive brutal PvP encounters, and build your legacy in a hostile world.

Underground Bunkers

Balanced economy system with black market traders, rare loot circulation, and progression-based gameplay.

Custom Events

Dynamic PvP hotspots, airdrops, bunker raids, and world events designed for high-risk gameplay.

Unique Mods

Custom tactical gear, sci-fi equipment, advanced weapons, and immersive survival mechanics.
